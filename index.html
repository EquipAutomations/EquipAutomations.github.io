ABOUT US.
  Equip
Equip is an automation company which provides solutions for work areas, homes and other complexes. Our finely engineered products and profound experience makes us paramount in this industry. We deliver products from smart work areas to top notch security systems. The elegant, modern and simple designs provide for an unparalleled experience to all our customers. In line with our responsibility to automate the community, our services are pocket friendly and extremely efficient.
