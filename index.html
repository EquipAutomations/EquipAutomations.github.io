ABOUT US.
  Equip
Equip is an automation company which provides solutions for work areas, homes and other complexes. Our finely engineered products and profound experience makes us paramount in this industry. We deliver products from smart work areas to top notch security systems. The elegant, modern and simple designs provide for an unparalleled experience to all our customers. In line with our responsibility to automate the community, our services are pocket friendly and extremely efficient.
   Vision
To be a community of people who are passionately advancing the use of technology to simplify processes in the resources around us. Endeavoring to operate harmoniously with the environment, in line with our responsibility to improve environmental performance, liaise with the social community and to implement a sustainable future for the generations to come.
   Mission
We strive to facilitate and accelerate automation, from homes to edifices, providing premium solutions at an affordable outlay to customers everywhere.
